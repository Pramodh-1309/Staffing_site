About Our Industries Division

Precision Staffing for Industries Excellence

At Aivirtrix, our Industries division is built for the demands of modern manufacturing and plant environments. We connect businesses with skilled, site-ready professionals who understand the pace, safety standards, and technical rigour of industrial operations.

From heavy manufacturing floors to specialised maintenance teams, we source, screen, and deploy talent that fits — not just on paper, but on the ground.

  • Candidates pre-screened for technical and hands-on competency
  • Safety certification verification on every placement
  • Capability to handle bulk and large-scale hiring drives
  • Both contract and permanent staffing options available
  • Fast deployment — screened candidates within days
  • Deep expertise across manufacturing, maintenance, and plant ops
